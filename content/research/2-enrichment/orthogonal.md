- **Prediction market miss ($0.01 four days before firing) + simultaneous Mullin announcement = COMPARTMENTALIZATION, not information asymmetry.** Decision was made in restricted circle. Either emergency authority genuinely bypassed notice requirements, or actions authorized under existing blanket authorities requiring no new notice. Absence should elevate procedural bypass probability.

This is operationalized policy cadence, not ad-hoc responses. Pattern suggests trade coercion is now STANDARD toolkit. Mullin inherits infrastructure.

- **GDELT coverage bursts (25 articles Feb 13-15 for ICE expansion) are AP wire pickup.** Query: Who sourced the story? If ICE directly, strategic disclosure (building public support). If leaked, opposition signaling. Source attribution matters for intent analysis.

### Absence of Expected Signals

- **No prediction markets on federal charges, Mullin confirmation, or contract continuation.** These are HIGH-VALUE questions with clear resolution criteria. Absence suggests market makers assess these as too uncertain (no informed traders) or too sensitive (legal risk). Organic probability estimates are UNANCHORED by market discipline.

- **Colombia coercion framing may be overweighted.** Colombia sent its own military plane to collect deportees. This suggests DIPLOMATIC CHANNEL existed, not pure economic desperation. Comtrade shows 38% petroleum dependency, but Colombia has non-US export markets. Reversal speed suggests negotiation, not pure coercion.

### Base Rate Violations

- **40% federal charges:** Historical perjury referral prosecution rate <10%. 40% is anchored to scandal severity, not DOJ prosecution patterns. Personal relationship denial is difficult to prove as willful perjury vs. definitional dispute.

- **80% advertising drop:** Government contracts are legal obligations. Cancellation requires breach finding, convenience termination (costly), or expiration + non-renewal. If contracts are annual (expire Sep 30), Q2 spending continues regardless. Probability is UNDERSPECIFIED without contract term structure.

### Magnitude Inconsistencies

- **$38.3B authorization treated as "10x scale change."** Multi-year authorization != current-year appropriation. Congress authorizes, Appropriations Committees fund. Multi-year authorizations routinely receive fractional appropriations. If FY2026 appropriation is $4-5B (vs $3.78B in FY2025), actual scale increase is 15-30%, not 10x.

- **Remittance leverage ($21.5B = 19% of GDP) is narrative plausibility, not mechanism.** Deportation volume (<1% of population) doesn't reduce remittances 1:1. Deportees may re-migrate (circular migration) or find domestic income. No evidence remittance flows actually dropped. Plausible mechanism, not validated mechanism.
